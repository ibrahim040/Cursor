# TTCD Management Synthesis — Revision Summary

This branch revises **TTCD Management Synthesis** after a thorough fact-check and reference
verification, and integrates checklists/content from the **TTCD Management Field Pocket Guide (RV)**.

## Deliverables
- `TTCD_Management_Synthesis_REVISED.docx` — the full revised synthesis. **All inserted or corrected
  text is shown in BOLD.** A complete itemized "List of Changes Made" is appended at the end of the
  document. Original figures are represented by captions with added source notes (the images live in
  the author's original Word file; the bold caption notes can be pasted in).
- `build_synthesis.py` — the generator used to produce the .docx (kept for reproducibility).

## Highlights of what changed

### A. Factual / statistical corrections (verified against primary sources)
- Construction fatalities 2018: "nearly 1,000" → **1,008** (BLS CFOI 2018).
- "more than three deaths per workday" → **"nearly three deaths every day"** (1,008 ÷ 365 ≈ 2.8).
- Added that transportation & warehousing had **874** fatalities in 2018 (second-highest after
  construction); rates 9.5 / 14.0 / 23.4 per 100,000 FTE verified.
- "overall industry average" → **"private-industry average"** (2.8, BLS SOII 2018).
- **Corrected "238 crashes involving large trucks or buses" → 215** fatal work-zone CMV crashes
  (~32%) in 2018 (FHWA-HOP-20-027). The 238 figure was unverifiable.
- 755 fatalities / 124 worker deaths attributed to the **National Work Zone Safety Information
  Clearinghouse (2019)** (the actual source).
- Removed/softened unverifiable claims (e.g., "ODOT 2020 measurable declines," "40–55% crash
  reduction," "within 100 ft … shown to cut worker strikes," "most work zone crashes occur during
  setup").
- Table 1: 70 mph taper cone spacing 60 ft → **70 ft** (MUTCD rule: spacing ft = speed mph).

### B. Standards updates
- **MUTCD date corrected from 2025 → 2023** (11th Edition, published Dec 19, 2023; effective Jan 18, 2024).
- **TMA crashworthiness updated from NCHRP 350 (and erroneous "NCHRP 488") → AASHTO MASH (2016)**,
  with the post-2019 manufacturing rule noted. New AASHTO (2016) reference added.
- Added **ATSSA (2025)** short-duration/short-term/mobile guidance.
- Replaced misattributed "NIOSH (2020)" hi-vis citation with **ANSI/ISEA (2020)**.

### C. Reference-list corrections
- Added missing: National Work Zone Safety Information Clearinghouse (2019), FHWA (2020) CMV plan,
  AASHTO MASH (2016), ATSSA (2025), Gambatese & Moeung Phase II (2024), Utility Work Zone Traffic
  Control Guidelines (2008).
- Reconciled the non-existent "Gambatese & Moeung, 2023" in-text citations (→ 2022 / 2024 Phase II).
- Removed duplicates: FHWA 2021 (×2) / Schroeder 2021 (same report); Theiss & Ullman 2017 (×2);
  TTI 2024 = Ullman et al. 2024; duplicate 23 CFR 630 entries.
- Split the merged Kansas DOT (2021) / Pennsylvania DOT (2020) entry into two.
- Kept previously-uncited Finley et al. (2017) by citing it in the short-duration section.
- Reframed Marjovi (2015) / Frydenberg (2021) as general convoy-control support; work-zone convoy
  claim re-attributed to Theiss et al. (2023, 2024).

### D. Figure source attributions
